Wellness and Meditation, fast.

This project was created with busy people in mind. To bring a little peace to an otherwise challenging schedule. Exercises within the app are brief and presented in a simple, clean design. Since it was designed with parents in mind, the focus is on rapid onboarding to get people situated quickly.

Research and Ideation

Research: Initial research involved a competitive analysis of some similar projects already on the market such as "8fit" and "Freeletics". Where those applications major on the benefits of physical exercise, BenOptim Wellness has a more personal feel. Ideation: Creating user personas and by extension user flows gives an idea of how an actual person might use the application in a real world setting.

Wireframe

Gray scale sketches are the first iterations of design and a good way to get ideas flowing on the layout and overall function of the application. This will include both mobile and desktop or tablet.

Design

At this point in the process I'm able to rapidly ideate and flesh the design out by beginning to incorporate colors, fonts, and themes, as well as use feedback from user testing to really try and find any existing pain points or glaring deficiencies in the application before moving to higher fidelity prototypes.

Prototype

Now I get to dial in the functionality and appearance of the application through mid and high fidelity prototype creation. My goal here is to bring the product to life and near-full functionality so this is where I will solidify things like any remaining areas of UI I want to incorporate and prepare to hand of the product to developers.

Conclusion

Final hand off to developers brings the process to a satisfactory close and provides my users with the product they've been waiting for!
